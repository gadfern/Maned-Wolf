Maned Wolf

These endangered species can run to up to speeds of 35 mph(55kph).

  • Scientific Name: Chrysocyon brachyurus
  • Lifespan: 6.5-15years
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Region: South America

According to the Smithsonian Institue, the maned wolf is the largest canid in South America. It looks more like a long-legged fox than a wolf. However, genetic studies have revealed they are neither fox nor true wolf, but a distinct species.
